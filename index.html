Saturday 28th Feb 2015, 8:30am - 9pm

Trafalgar Square

Trafalgar Square

We want to make Trafalgar square a truly immersive visitor experience through technology. Get visitors to engage and share their experience. Explore the art around them and appreciate the points of interest visible from Trafalgar Square.

Local Art - for visitors to London

Local Art - for visitors to London

Where do you start when planning to discover art in London? Why not create an experience that allows you to find great art based on where you are now or where you are staying. From street art to galleries to major exhibitions. If its on my doorstep I want to know about it.

Behind the hoardings

Behind the hoardings

Ever wonder what’s behind all the hoardings around the city? We want to create something that allows everyone to learn more and get involved. From using augmented reality targets and images / video to giving more information about the development.

Rent an Art Studio

Rent an Art Studio

Artists need space to create great work and many londoners have space available. From spare room’s, apartments, garages, shed’s etc everyone could benefit from knowing what is available and where. Submit your space, search and find based on your needs.

The Task
+

Working in teams of 4-6, create something amazing which can be an app, website, omni-channel experience, smart watch product or other tool. Once you follow the hackathon theme and base your idea around the following areas:

  • Connect London: Connecting London to make London smarter for all who live here and visit the city
  • Connect Art: Make art accessible, connect art and technology to bring art outside of galleries and streets
Tell Us About Yourself
+

To get the most out of the day we’re looking for a wide range of development skills so tell us a bit about yourself when you register and we can make sure we have a variety of people so that you can build the best team possible. When you register we're capturing a few details about the skills people will bring on the day so that we can provide the correct support for teams throughout the weekend.

What's Provided?
+

We’ll provide you with space to sit as a team, wifi access, comfortable break-out areas, drinks and snacks throughout the day and a host of supporting partners to make the day as comfortable, rewarding and fun as possible.

Refreshments & Food
+

There will be plenty of food and drinks available throughout the day - we guarantee you won’t go hungry. Please let us know any dietary requirements when you register.

Bring Your Own Laptop
+

This is a bring-your-own-laptop hackathon, this includes any chargers and anything else you need. Each team will need development environments and tools for web, android and/or iPhone app development, android/iPhone handsets, and the ability to deploy their apps to their handsets and other platforms. We're keen that developers, who take part on their own time, retain the IP that they create. However to generate the widest benefit from this event we encourage them to provide Open Source licences for the results of their creativity by creating a Public Repository on Github with an MIT license.

Prizes
+

Prizes will be awarded to the best concetps and end products, we will have multiple awards based on the following loose categories.

  1. Best product - most likely to be a commercial success
  2. Most innovative
  3. Best Connect Art idea
  4. Best Connect London idea
